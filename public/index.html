Welcome To Eastern Elementary School

At our school, students flourish in a kind and supportive environment. We focus on structuring proper class sizes and a curriculum that is both individualized while meeting if not exceeding accredited standards. Our teachers help students to reach their ultimate academic potential and succeed in achieving their goals.

Head of school's Welcome

Welcome to Eastern Elementary School where wisdom and knowledge shall be the stability of your times. We are a school that is committed to personal and corporate excellence. We provide a warm stable school environment based on an adaptation of the home school model. We are governed by the curriculum of Trinidad and Tobago but our school programs reflect inputs from international curricula since our students are being nurtured to manipulate the world environment. To this end we focus heavily on Information Technology and Financial Literacy, always being guided by Godly standards and Biblical principles. It is our belief that the home, school, church, and community share the responsibility of providing the appropriate foundation of knowledge, skills and attitudes that will equip each child to face the demands of the 21st Century. Our school colors are gold, blue and purple. Gold (yellow) represents generosity and elevation of the mind; blue represents truth and loyalty and purple represents royal majesty, sovereignty and justice. We advocate that even in a modern environment these traditional are values are essential. Once again welcome to EES where we are committed to excellence and where wisdom prevails. Sincerely, Susan Hypolite Head of School (B.Ed; Cert.Ed, TTT Dip)

Tuition

School fees for September 2018 - July 2019 academic year are: ​ ​ All class levels $12,000 ​ ​ During the school year, there will be additional fees for textbooks and extracurricular activities. ​ In collaboration with Eastern Aquatics Services swimming is also offered at a cost of $350 per term. ​ Tuition is billed three times a year and MUST be paid within the first week of each term.

Entrance Information

Eastern Elementary accepts all students, citizens and non citizens belonging to all societal backgrounds. Our institution is grounded in Christian values and the name Jesus Christ is revered. We accept students from 4+ to 11+ for the elementary school and students can progress automatically to Eastern Senior Academy for secondary level education. Students can also sit national SEA (Secondary Entrance Assessment) exam and move on to the secondary school of their choice. While we do not offer Special Education at present we accept students we have been recommended, after professional assessment, for mainstreaming. There is no entrance exam for admissions.
